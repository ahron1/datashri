Animated color map of COVID19 progress in Indian states

Date: 08 April 2020
Last update: 08 April 2020


As of 08 April, the number of reported COVID19 infections in India has crossed 5000. About 2 weeks back, this number was barely 400. Between 01 March and 04 April, the number of infections have doubled roughly every 4 days, with the rate of doubling having slowed down slightly in the next couple of days. We look into the details of the rate of growth on the other page linked here


Data on the number of COVID-19 cases in each state is taken from:
  • the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare website since 22 March.
  • Collated from various online media sources and tweets before 22 March.
